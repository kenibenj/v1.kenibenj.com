media


Media design and creation has long interested me, whether that be video & image editing, simple animations, or music production. Fair warning: I am still quite amateurish in my skills in all of these fields so do not expect anything too impressive. All the same, here is where I will post my completed media projects.

This is a living webpage; I will publish projects as I complete them.

  • The first song I ever produced, Pending was originally written back in December 2020. The song was made in FL Studio while the background animation was made with Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, and Photoshop. While little more than a simple tune, I'm still pretty happy with how this song turned out.


  • Written in Fall 2021, Ghosthunt is my second song and was also produced in FL Studio. A relatively simple Halloween-themed song(though it wasn't released until November), my goal with GhostHunt was to create a deeper and more fleshed-out piece as opposed to the incredibly simplistic nature of Pending. I also got to use Adobe Animate for the first time when creating the animation. I originally uploaded this song on a different channel shortly after its completion, but I rereleased it in July 2022 with some slight musical improvements and a better background animation.
